I am Andrei Legaspi, a Senior High School student with a deep passion for knowledge, inquiry, and the power of data to drive meaningful change. My academic journey has been shaped by a genuine curiosity about how the world works — not just on the surface, but at the level of patterns, systems, and human behavior.

I am planning to pursue a college degree in either Statistics or Psychology — two disciplines that share a common thread: the rigorous and thoughtful pursuit of truth. Whether it is analyzing datasets to uncover hidden trends or studying the complexities of the human mind, I am drawn to work that demands both critical thinking and genuine empathy.

10-Day Work Immersion Journal

Day 01 7:14 AM – 4:30 PM

Proposal Writing & Research

St. Edward School

Tasks Performed

Woke up at 5:20 AM, prepared, and arrived at St. Edward School at 7:10 AM. Learned how to properly write a formal proposal addressed to the school — covering the correct format, content, and professional tone. Conducted research by searching for Related Review of Literature (RRLs) relevant to the project, identifying credible sources, and organizing information effectively. Presented the proposal and RRLs to supervisors for review.

Learning Experiences

Learned the proper structure and tone required in a formal institutional proposal. Developed skills in identifying credible academic sources and organizing research information effectively. Gained an appreciation for clarity and professionalism in academic and institutional writing.

Workplace Values Observed

PunctualityDisciplinePatienceTeamwork

Challenges & Solutions

Finding reliable and updated sources for the RRLs was difficult — many articles were outdated or lacked credibility. Addressed this by filtering sources through trusted websites and academic references, and by seeking guidance from the supervisor.

Reflection & Insights

This day reinforced how important clarity and professionalism are in academic writing. It also deepened my appreciation for patience and teamwork when completing research-based tasks in a professional environment.

Day 02 7:00 AM – 4:30 PM

PowerPoint & Purchase Request

St. Edward School

Tasks Performed

Arrived at school at 7:10 AM, reviewed the assigned tasks, and prepared group materials. Created a PowerPoint presentation for the research proposal, accomplished a purchase request form for project materials, and studied basic statistics related to the research — including variable identification and simple data interpretation.

Learning Experiences

Learned how to organize information into clear and concise slides that are easy for an audience to follow. Gained an understanding of proper documentation procedures through the purchase request form, and improved knowledge of basic statistical concepts relevant to the research.

Workplace Values Observed

OrganizationTeamworkProfessionalism

Challenges & Solutions

Deciding which information to include in the slides was challenging since some parts of the proposal were too detailed. Addressed this by summarizing the key points and consulting groupmates to ensure that important ideas were still clearly represented.

Reflection & Insights

This day taught me how to balance completeness and conciseness when presenting ideas. It also highlighted the value of teamwork and collective decision-making in producing quality professional outputs.

St. Edward School

Precision matters deeply in every professional task. This day reminded me that small errors during planning can lead to large consequences later — a lesson that I will carry into all of my future work.
